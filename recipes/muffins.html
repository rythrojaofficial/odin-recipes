Muffins

An easy weekend morning snacktivity. What's not to love?

Ingredients

  • 2c (260g) flour
  • 1/2 c (100g) sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3/4 c (180ml) room temp Buttermilk or milk + splash of vinegar
  • 1/2 c (114g) unsalted butter, melted and cooled
  • 2 large eggs, room temperature
  • 1c blueberries

Instructions

  1. Prep
    • Take out eggs and milk to start warming
    • Add a splash of white vinegar to milk and stir once
    • Pre-heat Oven to 350F
    • melt 1/2 cup butter in oven or microwave
  2. Combine
    1. In a small bowl, whisk flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt
    2. In a medium bowl, whisk the milk, butter, and eggs until well combined
    3. Add the flour mixture and stir with a silicone spatula until just combined
    4. work blueberries into the mixture
  3. Bake
    1. Divide batter evenly between oiled muffin tins and redistribute blueberries if necessary
    2. Bake 20-25m or unitl a toothpick comes out with only a few moist crumbs
    3. Transfer muffins to wire rack to cool completely
