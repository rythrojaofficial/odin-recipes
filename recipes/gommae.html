Gommae Sauce

It's eating your veggies, if your veggies were like candy!

Ingredients

  • 1.5 pt almond
  • 1.5 pt toasted sesame seed
  • 1 pt dashi
  • 1 pt shoyu
  • 1 pt sugar

Instructions

  1. Toast almonds at 275F for 10m
  2. Stir dashi, shoyu, and sugar in pot on medium heat until sugar is fully dissolved, then set aside to cool
  3. Transfer almonds and sesame seeds to food processor and blitz until medium fine
  4. Add 1/3 of the liquid to food processor and blitz until combined
  5. Add the rest of the liquid and blend until well combined

Enjoy with any blanched greens, especially mustard greens! Gommae dressing will pair nicely with albacore tuna and wasabi for maximum deliciousness!
