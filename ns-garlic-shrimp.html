Northshore Garlic Shrimp

Spicy, garlicy, savory shrimpy goodness! A longtime local favorite! Of course we're talking about Oahu's own North Shore Style Garlic Shrimp! No trip to the North Shore is complete without a stop to the shrimp trucks! But what if you're craving it away from home?

Let's dive right in!

Ingredients

  • 12 oz shrimp, (deveined, shell-on)
  • 2 tbsp mochiko flour
  • 1 tbsp paprika
  • 1 tsp ceyenne pepper
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 head garlic, minced
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Cooking Instructions

  1. Coat the Shrimp
    1. devein, wash, and dry your shell-on shrimp
    2. combine 2 tbsp mochiko flour, 1 tbsp paprika, 1 tsp cayenne pepper, 1 tsp salt in medium mixing bowl
    3. toss and coat shrimp in dry rub
  2. Garlic Butter
    1. peel and mince at least one full head of garlic
    2. in a large pan, melt 4 tbsp butter over medium heat
    3. cook garlic until toasty and golden (around 3m)
    4. set aside in heat proof glass and salt+red pepper to taste
  3. Cook the Shrimp
    1. heat 1 tbsp olive oil on medium high heat
    2. cook shrimp in single layer batches 2m on each side or until crisp and browned
    3. pour garlic butter back into pan and mix with shrimp, cooking another minute
    4. plate over hot rice and squeeze lemon to finish
